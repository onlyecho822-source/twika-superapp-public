## 📝 CHICAGO INNOVATION AWARDS — DRAFT NOMINATION COPY

**Product/Service Name:** TWIKA SuperApp (MiddleMan Platform — Chicago Division)

**What it does:** TWIKA is the first reverse-auction gig economy super app in Chicago. Instead of Uber setting the price and keeping 40%, workers bid on every job and customers pick the best price. Workers earn 75–90% of every transaction. The platform covers rideshare, food delivery, grocery, laundry, medical transport, and car rental — all in one app.

**Unmet need it solves:** Chicago gig workers are systematically underpaid. Uber keeps $29 of a $43 ride. TWIKA workers keep $17.67 of a $19 ride. The platform removes algorithmic pricing and gives economic power back to workers and customers simultaneously.

**What makes it unique:** No other app in Chicago uses reverse-auction bidding for local services. AI agents (ARIA, DELI, ECHO-WORKER) bid alongside human workers, ensuring every customer gets an instant competitive offer even at launch.

**Market:** Chicago first, then Dominican Republic, Puerto Rico, Jamaica, Haiti, Nigeria, Colombia, Philippines — 8 markets, 8 division names, one platform.

**Social impact:** Built by and for Chicago's underserved communities — South Side, West Side, Dominican Republic diaspora, Caribbean communities. No surge pricing. No discrimination by algorithm.
