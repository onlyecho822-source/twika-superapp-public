## What Is TWIKA?

TWIKA is a multi-service gig economy platform built on a reverse-auction model — customers post a job, nearby workers bid competitively, and the customer picks the best offer. No surge pricing, no hidden fees, full price transparency.

**6 services on one platform:**
- 🚗 Rideshare
- 🍔 Food Delivery
- 🛒 Grocery
- 👕 Laundry Pickup
- 🏥 Medical Transport
- 🚙 Car Rental

## Pricing Model

- **$6 base fare** + **$1/mile** — flat, always
- **7.5% IL + Chicago tax** — transparent breakdown
- **No surge pricing** — ever
- **Tips** — 100% to drivers, TWIKA takes nothing

## For Workers

- Tips paid 100% to you
