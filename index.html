How It Works

Three Steps to the Best Price

The reverse-auction model means workers compete for your business — not the other way around.

1

📋 Post Your Job

Choose a service, enter pickup and destination. TWIKA auto-calculates a fair price: $6 base + $1/mile. No guessing. No surprises.

2

🔥 Workers Bid Live

Nearby workers see your job and submit their best price. Real competition, real savings. Bids stream in real time — watch them come in.

3

✅ You Pick the Best

Choose your worker manually or let our 30-second timer automatically accept the lowest valid bid. Pay securely through the app. Done.
