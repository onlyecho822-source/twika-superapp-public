## This Is Where People Take Back Control

For too long, the way people do business has been controlled by someone else.

A corporation decides what you get paid. An algorithm decides who sees your work. A platform takes 30%, 40%, sometimes 50% of every dollar you earn — and you have no say. You accept the terms or you don't eat.

**MiddleMan ends that.**

---

## What MiddleMan Is

MiddleMan is not an app. It is not a startup. It is not a tech company trying to disrupt an industry.

**MiddleMan is a platform built on one principle:**

> The person who needs the job done and the person who can do the job should be able to find each other — directly, fairly, and without interference.

We are the MiddleMan. Not because we take a cut and disappear. But because we stand between the problem and the solution and make sure the transaction is honest, fast, and complete.

We are the bridge. Not the toll booth.

---

## The Rules Are Simple

**No prejudice.**
It does not matter where you are from, what you look like, what language you speak, or what neighborhood you live in. If you can do the job, you can bid. If you need the job done, you can post. The work speaks for itself.

**No surge pricing.**
You post what you are willing to pay. Workers bid what they are willing to accept. The market decides — not an algorithm, not a corporation, not a surge multiplier at 2am on a Friday.

**No hidden fees.**
What you see is what you pay. What you bid is what you earn. MiddleMan takes a small, transparent commission. That is the deal. It does not change.

**Just get the job done.**
Correctly. In a timely manner. That is the only standard. Not your race. Not your accent. Not your zip code. Not your credit score. Can you do the job? Then do it.

This is for the Haitian mother who needs a babysitter she can trust, from her own community, at a price she can afford.

This is for every person who ever said: *"I just want to work. I just want to get paid fairly. I just want to do business on my own terms."*

**MiddleMan is for you.**

## The Standard

Every job posted on MiddleMan has one requirement:

**Get it done. Correctly. On time.**

Not because a corporation is watching. Not because an algorithm will penalize you. But because your reputation is your business. Because the person who hired you trusted you. Because that is what it means to work with integrity.

MiddleMan does not manage your integrity. It protects it.

## The Promise

To every worker on this platform:

You will be paid what you bid. You will not be deactivated without cause. You will not be replaced by a surge algorithm. You will not be invisible.

To every customer on this platform:

You will pay what you posted. You will get the job done. You will know exactly who is coming and what they will do. You will not be surprised.

To every community this platform serves:

We are not here to extract. We are here to connect. The money stays in the community. The work stays in the community. The trust stays in the community.

We are not the worker. We are not the customer. We are the floor they both stand on.
